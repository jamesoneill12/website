James O' Neill - PhD Student in Reinforcement Learning in Natural Language Understanding

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Insight Center for Data Analytics at the National University of Ireland, Galway. Unit for Natural Language Processing. I will be pursuing a PhD in Reinforcement Learning for Natural Language Understanding at the Computer Science department of University of Liverpool under the supervision of Dr. Danushka Bollegala.

My current research interests are discussed throughout a bi-weekly blog, and can be summarized as follows:

  • Machine Learning
    • Reinforcement Learning
    • Structured Prediction
    • Curriculum Learning and Imitation Learning
    • Transfer Learning/Covariate Shift and Multi-Task Learning
    • Graphical Convolution Neural Networks
    • Capsule Networks
    • Generative Modelling
    • MISO and MIMO modelling
  • Natural Language Processing
    • Nautral Language Understanding
    • Compositional Semantics and Natural Language Inference
    • Distribted Word Representations
    • Natural Language Generation
    • Question Answering
    • Learning to Rank
